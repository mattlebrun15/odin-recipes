Oranges

Home

Ingredients

  • Fresh oranges (as many as needed)
  • Optional: Sugar (for sweetening, if desired)
  • Optional: Cinnamon or chili powder (for extra flavor)
  • Optional: Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Peel the oranges: Using your hands or a knife, peel the outer skin of the oranges, removing all the pith (the white part). This will leave you with just the juicy fruit segments.
  2. Segment the oranges: Cut each orange into segments or "supreme" it by slicing between the membranes to get perfect, peel-free sections of orange.
  3. Optional – Sweeten: If you'd like to make the oranges sweeter, sprinkle a little sugar over the top of the segments. You can also try a dusting of cinnamon or chili powder for a unique twist.
  4. Optional – Garnish: Garnish with fresh mint leaves or other herbs for a fresh touch.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately as a refreshing snack, or use the segments in fruit salads, smoothies, or desserts.
