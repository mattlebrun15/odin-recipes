Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef, chicken, or pork (or your preferred protein)
  • 8-10 small corn tortillas
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1-2 avocados, sliced (optional)
  • 1-2 cups salsa or pico de gallo
  • 1 cup crumbled cotija cheese (optional)
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook the protein: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef, chicken, or pork, and cook until browned and cooked through, breaking it up as you go.
  2. Season the meat: Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until softened. Stir in the cumin, chili powder, paprika, cayenne (if using), salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to let the spices bloom.
  3. Warm the tortillas: Heat the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or on a griddle for about 30 seconds on each side, until warmed through and slightly charred.
  4. Assemble the tacos: Spoon the cooked meat mixture onto the warmed tortillas. Top with cilantro, salsa or pico de gallo, avocado slices, crumbled cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  5. Serve: Add hot sauce and extra lime wedges if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious street tacos!
